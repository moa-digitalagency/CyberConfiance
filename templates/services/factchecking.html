What is Fact-Checking?

Fact-checking is the process of verifying factual information to promote accuracy in reporting and combat misinformation. We use advanced OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) techniques combined with VirusTotal security scanning to ensure both accuracy and safety.

Our OSINT Methodology

Open Source Intelligence involves collecting and analyzing publicly available information from various sources. Our process includes verification of sources, cross-referencing multiple databases, and security scanning to protect our clients from malicious content.
