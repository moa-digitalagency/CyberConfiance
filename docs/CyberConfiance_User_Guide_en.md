# User Guide - CyberConfiance

## 1. Overview

CyberConfiance is your one-stop portal to check the security of your files, links, and personal data. The interface is designed to be simple and intuitive.

---

## 2. Using Analysis Tools

### 2.1. File and Link Analyzer
Accessible from the home page or the "Tools" menu.

1.  **URL**: Paste a suspicious link into the text field. Click "Analyze". The system will check if the site is known to host viruses or phishing.
2.  **File**: Click "Choose a file" (Max 50MB). The file is uploaded, scanned by 70 antivirus engines, then deleted from our servers.
3.  **Results**: A report is displayed with a color code (Green = Safe, Red = Danger).

### 2.2. QR Code Analyzer
1.  Take a photo of the QR Code or upload a screenshot.
2.  The tool decodes the hidden link and analyzes it BEFORE you open it.
3.  If the link is safe, you can click to open it.

### 2.3. Email Leak Check
1.  Go to the "Quiz & Leaks" menu.
2.  Enter your professional or personal email address.
3.  The system queries the global database of leaks (HIBP).
4.  If your email is compromised, the list of affected sites is displayed. **Change your passwords immediately.**

---

## 3. The Cybersecurity Quiz

Test your knowledge in 5 minutes.
*   Answer 15 multiple-choice questions.
*   Each answer is explained to help you learn.
*   At the end, get your score and a certificate of achievement (if > 80%).

## 4. Citizen Services

### 4.1. Fact-Checking
Received doubtful information on WhatsApp?
*   Use the "Fact-Check" form.
*   Copy the text or import the image.
*   Our experts will verify the information.

### 4.2. Reporting
To report a scam or a malicious site, use the anonymous reporting form. Your data is protected.
